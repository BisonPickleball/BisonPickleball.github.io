Welcome to Bison Pickleball

Winnipeg’s Premier Pickleball Clinics & Lessons!

At Bison Pickleball, we’re dedicated to growing a thriving pickleball community in Winnipeg and Manitoba. Whether you’re a beginner looking to learn the game or an experienced player aiming to refine your skills, our learn-to-play sessions and skills-and-drills clinics are designed to help you improve, compete, and have fun!

With over 4 years of experience, we have successfully run more than 150 pickleball clinics in Winnipeg and Manitoba and taught over 2,000 students—helping players of all levels develop confidence, improve technique, and master game strategy. Led by our NCCP Level 2-certified head instructor, our expert-led clinics focus on fundamentals, advanced techniques, and strategic play, ensuring a structured and engaging learning experience.

We offer pickleball clinics in Winnipeg and Manitoba both indoors and outdoors, providing flexibility and comfort for all participants. Additionally, we can organize group bookings for birthday parties and corporate events, making your special occasions fun and memorable. We also provide paddles and balls for anyone to use during our clinics, so you don't need to worry about bringing your own equipment.

We have run clinics in multiple small towns, including Arborg, Brandon, Selkirk, Île-des-Chênes, Stonewall, Winnipeg Beach, East St. Paul, Winkler, Morden, and Pinawa, and we are willing to travel to provide clinics in other locations as well.

Subscribe to our clinic mailing list so we can contact you when we offer new pickleball clinics in Winnipeg and Manitoba that you might be interested in hearing about.
