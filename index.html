Bison Pickleball — Elevate Your Game

At Bison Pickleball, we’re committed to growing a strong and welcoming pickleball community across Winnipeg and Manitoba. Whether you’re new to the sport or an experienced player looking to sharpen your skills, our learn‑to‑play sessions and skills‑and‑drills clinics are designed to help you improve, compete, and have fun.

With more than 300 clinics delivered and over 4,000 players taught, we’ve built a proven track record of helping players develop confidence, refine technique, and elevate their game strategy.

Coaching Team

Our programs are led by an NCCP Level 2–certified head instructor who is also a DUPR‑certified coach and a 5.0+ DUPR player. Our assistant instructor is also 5.0+ DUPR player who can effectively demonstrate all techniques being taught.

Clinic Structure

We maintain a 1:6 instructor‑to‑student ratio for most clinics to ensure personalized attention. Clinics are capped to match available court space, with no more than 4 players per court so everyone stays active with no waiting around.

Logistics

We offer clinics both indoors and outdoors across Manitoba and Saskatchewan. We also organize group sessions for birthday parties, corporate events, and community gatherings. All necessary equipment—including paddles and balls—is provided.

Communities Served

Arborg, Brandon, East St. Paul, Falcon Lake, Gimli, Île‑des‑Chênes, La Salle, Morden, Niverville, Pinawa, Regina, Saskatoon, Selkirk, Stonewall, Winkler, Winnipeg, and Winnipeg Beach.

We are always open to travelling to new locations.
